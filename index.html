Pokemon Mart: Come and Spend Your Hard Earn Cash

  • Name:

    Pokeball

    This type of Poké Ball is the weakest and the only type that is typically available at the beginning of the Pokémon games. It has a catch rate modifier of ×1 in the catch formula.

    Price:

    $200


  • Name:

    Quick Ball

    It can be used to catch a wild Pokémon, being more likely to succeed if used at the start of the encounter.

    Price:

    $800


  • Name:

    Masterball

    It can be used to catch a wild Pokémon without fail. It was invented by the Silph Co.

    Price:

    $1600
